My Traxxas Slash 4x4

Above is one iteration of my Sl4sh.
Aftermarket body shell from Amazon Summitlink USA Flag Shell



After breaking a stock A-arm, I upgraded to the heavy duty A-arms-

Proline Extended Body Mounts and 2.8 Sand Paw tires for snow and sand bashing, as well as 2.8 Trenchers for beast-mode!

The Guts

I custom fabricated quarter inch thick polycarbonate mounting plates to accommodate repositioning the ESC and the receiver box.
I also used a barrel sander to grind the battery compartment sidewalls to fit two 2S batteries standing on edge.

Internal components that I've upgraded
Castle 1415 2400kV brushless paired with Mamba X ESC ----- Castle Creations
Tekno Big Bone center driveshaft and outdrives ----- Tekno RC
Traxxas GTR shocks- GTR Shocks for front -------- GTR Shocks for rear
Homemade serial XT60 adapter for 4S power
Zeee 2S LiPO batteries (surprisingly high quality and low cost!) ----- Amazon.com
