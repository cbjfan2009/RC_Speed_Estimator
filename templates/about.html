4x4 RC trucks are an excellent hobby to get involved in.

My Traxxas Slash 4x4

Pictured above is one iteration of my Sl4sh.

I entered the hobby in 2020 after years of wanting to buy a hobby-grade RC truck. I enjoyed watching trophy truck and rally racing, so I picked a 4x4 trophy truck-style platform. The Traxxas Slash 4x4 fit the bill. Based on great reviews and wide variety of parts, I knew I'd be able to keep upgrading over time to keep the hobby fresh. I had toy-grade RCs growing up, but had very little experience tuning and fixing broken RCs. After breaking a stock A-arm, I dipped my toes into the waters of fix/replace/upgrade maintenance. I upgraded to the Heavy-Duty A-arms.
Slowly, I added upgrades like:

  • Castle 1415 2400kV brushless paired with Mamba X ESC ----- Castle Creations
  • Tekno Big Bone center driveshaft and outdrives ----- Tekno RC
  • Traxxas GTR shocks- GTR Shocks for front -------- GTR Shocks for rear
  • Homemade serial XT60 adapter for 4S power
  • Zeee 2S LiPO batteries (surprisingly high quality and low cost!) ----- Amazon.com


Proline Extended Body Mounts and 2.8 Sand Paw tires for snow and sand bashing, as well as 2.8 Trenchers for beast-mode!
